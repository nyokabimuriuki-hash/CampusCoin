About CampusCoin

CampusCoin is a web-based budgeting system developed for university students. It supports daily expense recording, income tracking, monthly financial reporting, and simple account management through role-based access.

The system was designed to reduce the difficulty students face when trying to monitor how much money they spend over time. It provides a simple user interface, fast data entry, and visual feedback through reports.
